About

This tool would not exist without the amazing work from the following:

  • ColorMusic® — Created by Mike George and the inspiration behind this tool.

  • Verovio — Open-source library for converting .mei files to SVG.

  • Music Encoding Initiative (MEI) — Open-source effort for encoding musical documents in a machine-readable structure.

  • musicXML™ — Enables reliable interchange of Western music notation between software tools.

  • BeautifulSoup — For parsing/manipulating .mei and .svg files.

The ColorMusic Notation Tool is provided for your playing pleasure by Jake Kirsch, a data engineer based in Atlanta.


The tool is free to use. If it helps you, please consider supporting hosting & development costs ☕
